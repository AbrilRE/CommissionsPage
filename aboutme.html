Hi, i'm Kimchi!

Since i was a kid i was really interested on everything that was related to art, my mom says i always drew on everything, and by everything i really mean everything(even my parents furniture).

When i was in middle school, i got so many attention calls because i was always drawing. That's when i got my first tablet, i was 13 and everything started more seriously, i practiced really hard.

Years passed by and i decided it was time to try selling my art and i was really surprised, people really liked what i created, i was really happy to help them bring their ideas to life!

Things i'm interested

logos, web design and game design

To be honest i'm really interested on anything related to design. I've designed some personal games, logos and web pages, most of them for school, i'm looking foward to learn more so i can offer this kind of commissions.

My goals

create something people never forget

All i've ever thought when it comes to my goals, has always been leave mark of what i did here in this world. I don't really care if i create a game, a comic or even just illustrations, i want them to see what i've done and feel inspirated to create their own things.

My dreams

be part of a community of my own

As said, i've always dreamed to see people enjoy what i created, that's why having a community of my own seems really attractive to me, i would love to read what people think about it what they create inspired by i've done.
