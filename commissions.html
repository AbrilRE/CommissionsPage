Terms & conditions

After chosing your commissions type, here is some information you should be aware of before commissioning me. Almost everything that you need to know is here, if there is anything you wanna know, please feel free to ask!
Rules
  • Commissions are not for commercial use (If you are interested, please send me an email, so we can talk about it).
  • The prices are NON-negotiable.
  • I'll upload to my gallery the drawing you commissioned me, so i can use it as part of my portfolio.
  • If character clothes are too complex the price could go up.
  • Detailed background it's not included.
  • Two free changes. (Not applied to drastical changes after the finished drawing).
  • I'll deliver you 3 different files: transparent background, white background and color background.
  • No refunds.
Extra details
  • Color or patern background is included (If you want any specific color or patern please let me know).
  • Detailed background price depends on complexity (around 5usd to 20usd).
  • Adding more characters to the drawing is +50% of the commission price per character (This means, if the commission it's $20, adding one more character would be +$10).
Payment
  • Payment only throught aypal or Ko-fi
  • Payment must be sent first (After we discuss details).
  • Can also be 50/50 , first half after ordering the commission an the other half when i notify you i finished it (I'll send you a watermarked image, so you can be sure i did it, when i confirm i received the paymet i will send you the commission without the watermark).
I want a commission, what's next?
  • You can send me an email with the commission type, reference images of the character(s) (if you have more than one, it would be perfect) and the rough idea of it.
  • I can work with description, mood board and reference photos.
  • I'll reply as soon as possible with my payment info to start working immediately.
  • I'll keep you updated with wips of it (You can also indicate changes throught this wips).
  • When i finish it i'll send you a message.
